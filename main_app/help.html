Payments & Support

What should I do if my payment fails?
If your payment fails, first check your internet connection and ensure your payment details, like card number or UPI ID, are entered correctly. Retry the payment after a few minutes. If the issue persists, use an alternative payment method or contact your bank to confirm there are no restrictions on your account. If the payment still doesn’t go through, please reach out to our support team for further assistance.
How long does it take for payments to reflect?
Payments typically reflect immediately after a successful transaction. However, in some cases, it can take up to 24 hours due to banking or processing delays. If your payment does not reflect within this timeframe, contact us with your transaction reference number, and we’ll investigate the issue promptly.
What if my refund or settlement is delayed?
Refunds or settlements are usually processed within 5-7 business days. If the amount is not credited within the expected time, check your bank account or payment gateway. Occasionally, there may be slight delays due to bank processing times. Please share your transaction details with our team so we can escalate the issue and ensure it’s resolved as quickly as possible.
How can I contact support for payment issues?
For payment-related issues, you can contact us via: Email: support@yourwebsite.com Live Chat: Available on our website during business hours.
